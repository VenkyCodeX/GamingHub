Rental Terms & Policies

1. Eligibility

  • Must be 18+ years old
  • Valid government ID required (Aadhar/Passport/DL)
  • Hyderabad residents only

2. Security Deposit

A refundable security deposit is required at the time of pickup. Amount varies by product category.

3. Rental Period

  • Minimum rental: 7 days
  • Maximum rental: 90 days
  • Extensions available on request

4. Damage Policy

Any damage beyond normal wear will be charged. Insurance add-on covers accidental damage up to ₹5000.

5. Late Returns

Late returns are charged at the daily rate. Please inform us in advance if you need an extension.

6. Cancellation

  • Free cancellation up to 24 hours before pickup
  • 50% refund for cancellations within 24 hours
  • No refund after pickup
